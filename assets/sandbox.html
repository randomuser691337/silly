Sandbox

WebDesk Sandbox is a safe place to run anything, without any consequences.

Everything in Sandbox is temporary and will be destroyed on exit. WebDesk Security has been disabled in Sandbox as well.

To exit Sandbox: Open the Everything Menu in the sandbox and click "Exit Sandbox."

To keep Sandbox running in the background but hidden: Open the Everything Menu and click "Suspend Sandbox."

Also note that Sandbox is also a variant of WebDesk Beta.
